# Discord Music Bot

Discord Bot
<br>- Plays music or the audio of any video published on YouTube
 
Many Commands
<br>  /play
<br>  /skip
<br>  /skipto
<br>  /queue
<br>  /loop
<br>  /info
<br>  /pause
<br>  /quit
<br>  /resume
<br>  /shuffle
  
Must be in voice channel for bot to join!
